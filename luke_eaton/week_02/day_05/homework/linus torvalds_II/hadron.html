:~$The Large Hadron Collider

~Basically the aim of the LHC is to allow physicists to test the predictions of different theories of particle physics, including measuring the properties of the Higgs boson (Higgs boson, also known by its nickname the "God particle,"an invisible energy field present throughout the universe that imbues other particles with mass.) and searching for the large family of new particles predicted by supersymmetric theories, as well as other unsolved questions of physics..
